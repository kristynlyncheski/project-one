Objective

Roll dice for scoring combinations and get the highest total possible!

How to Play

On your turn, you may roll the dice up to 3 times, although you may stop and score after your first or second roll.

The player to get the highest scoring combination of the 13 categories wins. The game ends when both players have filled in all 13 plays.

First roll: Roll all 5 dice. Click any dice you want to keep, or select a move in the scorecard to end your turn.

Second roll: Reroll the dice you didn't click. Click any dice you want to keep, or select a move in the scorecard to end your turn.

Third roll: Reroll the dice you didn't click. You must then select a move in the scorecard.

Scoring

If your upperscore total is 63 or more, you get a 35-point bonus.
